# CCG SiteSpark: Social Media Master Strategy
**Goal:** Generate 5-10 qualified leads per month from LinkedIn and Facebook by March 2026.

---

## Part 1: The Strategic Foundation

### Your Competitive Advantage
You are **NOT** competing with Wix, Squarespace, or Fiverr.  
You are competing with **the status quo** (businesses doing nothing or using terrible DIY sites).

**Your Message:**
> "We stop local service businesses from losing emergency jobs to competitors with outdated websites."

**Your Proof Points:**
1. Industry-specific demos (PlumberDemo, ElectricianDemo, etc.)
2. Local Brooklyn presence (721 Avenue U)
3. Speed to market (sites delivered in 7-14 days, not months)

## Part 4: Engagement Tactics (Daily Actions)

### LinkedIn (15 mins/day)
1. **Search:** `Owner OR Founder` in `Construction, Plumbing, HVAC` within `50 miles of Brooklyn, NY`
2. **Connect:** Send 5 personalized connection requests:
   ```
   Hey [Name], I help Brooklyn-area [Industry] businesses get more leads online. 
   Always connecting with local business owners. Looking forward to your content!
   ```
3. **Engage:** Comment on 3 posts from your target audience. Be helpful, not salesy.

### Facebook (10 mins/day)
1. **Join Groups:** Request to join 1 new local business group per week.
2. **Engage:** Comment on 2 posts in your groups. Answer questions if possible.
3. **Share Value:** Once per week, post a "Free Tip" in a group (not your page).

## Part 5: Success Metrics (Track Weekly)

### Leading Indicators (Activity):
- [ ] 3 LinkedIn posts published
- [ ] 4 Facebook posts published
- [ ] 25 new LinkedIn connections
- [ ] 10 meaningful comments on other posts
- [ ] 1 Facebook group post

### Lagging Indicators (Results):
- Website clicks from social (check GA4 Traffic Acquisition)
- Profile views (LinkedIn Analytics / Facebook Insights)
- Inbound messages/connection requests
- Contact form submissions mentioning "saw your post"

**Goal:** By Week 4, you should see 50-100 website clicks per week from social.

## Part 8: When to Pivot

If after 30 days you haven't seen:
- At least 1 meaningful conversation in DMs
- At least 50 website clicks from social
- At least 5 positive reactions per post

**Then adjust:**
- Try video content (30-second screen recordings of your demos)
- Join different Facebook groups
- Test posting at different times
- Go MORE visual (infographics, before/after screenshots)

## TLDR: Your Weekly Workflow

**Monday Morning (30 mins):**
- Write 3 LinkedIn posts + 4 Facebook posts for the week
- Schedule them in Meta Business Suite / LinkedIn native scheduler

**Daily (15 mins):**
- Engage: Comment on 5 posts
- Connect: Send 5 LinkedIn requests
- Check: Who viewed your profile? Send a follow-up DM.

**Friday Afternoon (20 mins):**
- Check Google Analytics: Which posts drove traffic?
- Adjust next week's content based on what worked.

**That's it. Social media is a marathon, not a sprint.**

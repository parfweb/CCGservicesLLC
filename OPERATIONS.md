# CCG SiteSpark: Agency Operations Manual

## 1. Core Communication (Email & Admin)
**Recommended Provider:** Google Workspace (Business Starter)
*   **Cost:** ~$6/user/month
*   **Why:** Best spam filtering, calendar integration, and professional appearance.
*   **Setup required:**
    1.  Sign up at [workspace.google.com](https://workspace.google.com)
    2.  Verify domain ownership (TXT record in DNS).
    3.  Update MX records (Google guides you through this).
*   **Users:**
    *   `hello@ccgsitespark.com` (Shared alias/group for generic inquiries)
    *   `[YourName]@ccgsitespark.com`
    *   `[CofounderName]@ccgsitespark.com`

## 2. Website Infrastructure
*   **Hosting:** Netlify (connected to GitHub)
    *   *Status:* **Live & Auto-Deploying**
    *   *Cost:* Free (Starter Tier)
*   **Code Repository:** GitHub
    *   *Access:* Invite your co-founder as a "Collaborator" to the repo if they need to see code.

## 3. Client Management (CRM)
*   **Inbounds:** Currently arriving via Netlify Forms -> Email.
*   **Next Step:** Connect a tool like **HubSpot** (Free Tier) or **Monday.com**.
    *   *Why:* Track who you called, who ignored you, and who is ready to buy.

## 4. Sales Assets
*   **Showcase:** Use the `/industry-web-design` pages (e.g., `/plumber-web-design`) during sales calls.
*   **Demo Mode:** Open the site on a tablet/phone to show the "Mobile First" behavior. Clients love this.
